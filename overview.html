What is HarvestMind

Decision intelligence
for the modern farmer

HarvestMind is an AI-powered engine that helps farmers make smarter decisions — whether to pivot crops, persevere through market dips, or harvest early before prices crash. Powered by GLM reasoning from ilmu.ai.

01
Market Analysis
Tracks crop price trends and oversupply signals to prevent losses at harvest time.
02
Weather Intelligence
Accounts for rainfall, drought, and seasonal volatility when giving recommendations.
03
Pivot vs Persevere
Clear actionable decisions — not just predictions. Tells you exactly what to do and when.
04
Risk Hedging
Recommends crop diversification strategies to reduce income risk across seasons.

Make informed decisions that protect your income, reduce waste, and strengthen your farm's resilience.
